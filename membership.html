Members Matter


Your membership contribution to the Speed Art Museum makes a big impact. Members provide critical operating support so we can invite everyone to celebrate art.

Membership is fully tax-deductible as allowed by law with the following exceptions: all but $75 of the Supporter, Silver, Gold, and Platinum levels are tax-deductible.

General Membership discounts of 10% for Seniors, Military Personnel, and Students and 30% for Teachers are available on-site and by phone. Member discounts on gift memberships are only available on-site and by phone.

For more information, call the Membership Coordinator at 502.634.2719 or email membership@speedmuseum.org.

NEW!


Non-profit 501(c)3 organizations may purchase a Non-Profit Membership to the Speed Art Museum for $250.

The membership provides: 50 general admission passes to the Museum, to be used within one year of purchase.

To purchase a Non-Profit Membership, you must call LaShana Avery at 502.634.2719.

Restrictions: Passes may be used for individuals or in groups. Groups of 10 or more must schedule a group tour. Tours may be self-guided or docent-led. To schedule group tours, contact tours@speedmuseum.org. If you have scheduled a group tour and additional general admission passes are required, they may be purchased at the group rate of $10 per person. General admission passes are good during regular hours when the museum is open to the public. Visitors using the pass may attend any program that is free with general admission. The general admission passes do not entitle the holder to other Members-only events or benefits.
